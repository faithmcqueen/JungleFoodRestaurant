About

Jungle food is a latin-american based restaurant located in three different jungles in the world. We pride ourselves on our customer experience which provides customers with quality food that is based solely on natural ingredients picked from the nature around us. We are currently located in the Amazon Jungle, the Lacandon Jungle and the Osa Peninsula Jungle, with the possibility of various expansions.

A family

Jungle food’s journey started as a simple family dream for the Jung’s family that lived near the Amazon jungle. Most of their daily food was a fusion between Peru’s typical plates and a variety of eatable greens they found near their house.

A House

On 2012 the Jung family saw an opportunity to share their food creations with their community and decided to open a small family owned restaurant on their own house.

A Restaurant

On 2015, as years progressed they soon realized they could not hold all of the people in their own home. So they built the first Jungle Food restaurant, made up of two floors and 15 employess.

A Movement

In 2017, as Jungle food in the became more popular, the Jung's decided to Franchise their idea and expand it to other jungles, so similar communities could enjoy simple food made up with fresh ingredients of the jungle.
